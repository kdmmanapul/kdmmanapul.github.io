Who Am I

Hello! My name is Kurt Manapul

I'm a software developer with good experience on developing Mobile Applications, Web Applications and Games utilizing Javascript. I had worked on multiple projects and companies regarding E-Com sites, Social Networking Apps, Logistics App and Branding.

What I do

I take pride in turning your ideas into digital realities. With personalized attention and expertise, I help your business stand out and create exceptional digital experiences. Trust me to elevate your brand and make a lasting impact.

Mobile Development

With expertise in mobile app development, I craft engaging and user-friendly applications for both iOS and Android platforms. From concept to deployment, I deliver top-notch mobile solutions that drive customer engagement and satisfaction.

Website Application Development

I excel in creating dynamic and functional web applications. Whether you need an e-commerce platform, content management system, or a custom web solution, I provide tailored development services to meet your specific requirements.

UI/UX Design

Specialize in creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces (UI) and user experiences (UX). Focus on designing interfaces that are both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly, ensuring an engaging and seamless user journey.

Frontend Design

With a keen eye for detail and a strong command of frontend technologies, I excel in frontend design. From HTML and CSS to JavaScript frameworks, I craft responsive and interactive designs that bring websites and applications to life.

Marketing

I understand the importance of effective marketing strategies. I offer comprehensive marketing services, including digital marketing campaigns, SEO optimization, and social media management, to help your business reach its target audience and drive growth.

Brand Identity

I help businesses establish a unique and memorable brand identity. From logo design to visual branding elements, I create cohesive and impactful brand identities that resonate with your target audience.
